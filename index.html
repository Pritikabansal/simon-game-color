- Instructions for Playing the Simon Game -

1. Start the Game: Tap or Press any key to begin the game. The game will generate a random sequence of colors.

2. Watch the Sequence: Pay close attention as the game highlights a sequence of colors on the game board. Each color will light up in a specific order.

3. Repeat the Sequence: After the sequence is displayed, it's your turn to repeat it by clicking the buttons in the same order. The game board buttons correspond to the colors in the sequence.

4. Progress to the Next Level: If you successfully repeat the sequence, you will progress to the next level. The game will add one more color to the sequence, making it longer and more challenging.

5. Continue Playing: Continue watching and repeating the sequences as they get longer with each level. Try to remember the order of colors and click the buttons in the correct sequence.

6. Game over: The game is over if you click the wrong button or fail to repeat the sequence correctly. Your final score will be displayed, indicating the highest level you reached.

7. Play Again: Tap/Press any key to play again and try to beat your previous score!
